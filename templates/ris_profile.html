ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Reece, and I’m an engineering student who’s passionate about exploring the intersection of creativity and technology. I’ve always been fascinated by the way simple ideas can evolve into powerful solutions, whether it’s through building circuits, coding applications, or designing innovative systems. As an aspiring engineer, I’ve had the chance to dive into hands-on projects that have taught me a lot. I’ve coded interactive tools using Python, experimented with hardware like Arduino and Raspberry Pi, and even participated in a hackathon where my team designed a smart energy monitor. Each experience has deepened my love for combining technical skills with practical problem-solving. When I’m not immersed in tech, you’ll find me playing guitar, sketching designs, or exploring local food spots with friends. I believe life is about balance—creativity, collaboration, and discovery. To me, engineering is more than just equations; it’s a way to bring ideas to life and make a difference in the world.
